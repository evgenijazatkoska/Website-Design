Norway is the westernmost, northernmost—and in fact the easternmost—of the three Scandinavian countries. Best known for the complex, deep and breathtakingly beautiful fjords along its west coast. Norway shares borders with Sweden, Finland and Russia on the east side, and an extensive coastline facing the North Atlantic Ocean on the west side. Here the climate is wet and mild compared to the east and the north, where winters are colder and longer.

Norway is well known for its amazing and varied scenery, the jaw-dropping fjords in the west of the country and the Lofoten islands in the North. Norway’s main destinations include everything from the urban vibe of Oslo to the wide views at The North Cape.

The trip to Norwegian Fjords offers a perfect combination of exploring city and nature in breathtaking sceneries of Southern Norway! See the dramatic difference between Norwegian cities and small rustic fishing villages, take a boat tour through the amazing fjords and hike in spectacular landscapes of the Voss region. Try hiking followed by cosy relaxing time in the small picturesque villages of Flåm and Gudvangen. This journey is the perfect combination of city and nature exploration.

If you are seeking unforgettable experiences in nature far above the Arctic circle, Lofoten island is the place to visit! Wild Norwegian nature with its majestic mountains and picturesque fjords offers a perfect setting for an adventure in the land where vikings once lived. We will visit charming fisherman villages and make stops for pictures in the most iconic places of Lofoten.
